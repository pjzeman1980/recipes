Ingredients:
2 c. pumpkin seeds (cleaned)
1 T. butter
Salt

Directions:
 1. Preheat over to 350 degrees.  
 2. Melt butter in microwave.  Add pumpkin seeds to bowl and toss with 
    melted butter.
 3. Spread pumpkins out on baking sheet, sprinkle with salt to taste.
 4. Bake pumpkin seeds for 45 minutes, turning every 15 minutes
 5. Add any additional salt to taste
